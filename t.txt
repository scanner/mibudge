Okay. This is "mibudge" which kind of stands for "my budget." It is a project I started and never finished. It has come time to resurrect this and with claude's help finish this.

This project is an attempt to replicate and improve for my and my family's use the budgeting model that the online bank simple.com had achieved.

The goal of this service is to help budget, refine, control, and plan expenditures.
